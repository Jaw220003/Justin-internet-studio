Favorite Movies

These movies that are listed are known be my favorites as to liking a mixure of action pack, comedy, little bit of drama, and cartoons that express who I am today. Many people might not get the references or never seen the movies I watched growing up, but I treasure these as the way of enjoying the outside world.

Spider-man, centers on student Peter Parker (Tobey Maguire) who, after being bitten by a genetically-altered spider,
gains superhuman strength and the spider-like ability to cling to any surface.

Hulk is a 2003 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics, as scientist Bruce Banner, whose inner demons transform him in the aftermath of a catastrophic experiment.

Bad Boys Bad Boys, what you gonna do? Two hip detectives protect a witness to a murder while investigating a case of stolen heroin from the evidence storage room from their police precinct.

Madea goes to jail, The film tells the story of Madea going to prison for her uncontrollable anger management problems as she befriends a young incarcerated prostitute.

Bad Boys 2, The drug ecstasy is flowing into Miami, and the police want it stopped. Police Detective Marcus Burnett and his partner, Mike Lowrey, are just the men to do it.

Surf's Up, Surfing means everything to teenage penguin Cody Maverick (Shia LaBeouf). Followed by a documentary film crew, he leaves his home in Antarctica for Pen Gu Island, site of the the Big Z Memorial Surf Off.

Cars, In a world where the towns are peopled with cars, and even the bugs are mini motors, a young ambitious racing car called Lightning McQueen is a star. On his way to an important event he has to stop in the little town of Radiator Springs after he accidentally damages the road and is forced to repair it.

Ponyo, during a forbidden excursion to see the surface world, a goldfish princess encounters a human boy named Sosuke, who gives her the name Ponyo. Ponyo longs to become human, and as her friendship with Sosuke grows, she becomes more humanlike.
