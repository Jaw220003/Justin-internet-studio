Statement Design

The design statement for Bikini Bottom in SpongeBob SquarePants is rooted in creator Stephen Hillenburg's background in marine biology and is best described as a whimsical, "found-object" aesthetic that blends elements of a typical American city with quirky, aquatic-themed architecture and vibrant, unrealistic colors.
