Places To Eat

Krusty Krab

The Krusty Krab is a fictional fast-food restaurant in the animated show SpongeBob SquarePants,
owned by the greedy Eugene H. Krabs, which serves the popular Krabby Patty.
The restaurant is known for its rivalry with the Chum Bucket, owned by Plankton,
who constantly tries to steal the secret formula for the Krabby Patty. SpongeBob works as the fry cook, while Squidward is the cashier,
and the building is shaped like a lobster trap.

Weenie Hut Juniors

Weenie Hut Jr.'s is a restaurant in SpongeBob SquarePants that is a safe and "soft" alternative to the tough establishment, Salty Spitoon. It serves as a hangout for characters deemed "weenies,"
weak, or nerdy, and is known for its weenie-themed menu, robot servers, and various special days. The restaurant's name implies it's for children, but it also serves as a place for those considered weak or not tough enough for the Salty Spitoon.

Fancy!

"Fancy!" can refer to several things in SpongeBob SquarePants, most commonly the high-class restaurant where characters go for expensive meals. It can also refer to the general idea of opulence and high society,
particularly as represented by the rival character Squilliam Fancyson, who lives a life of luxury. The term is used to describe an environment of wealth and exclusivity, contrasting with the usual, more common settings like the Krusty Krab.
