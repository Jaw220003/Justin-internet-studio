Bikini Bottom

Bikini Bottom is a fictional underwater city in the SpongeBob SquarePants cartoon, while the real-life island is Bikini Atoll, located in the Marshall Islands in the Pacific Ocean

Highlight/Facts

  • A-bomb origin: Series creator Stephen Hillenburg, a former marine biologist, named the city after Bikini Atoll, a site for U.S. nuclear weapons testing between 1946 and 1958.
  • Urban layout: The main road, Conch Street, runs through the city, passing the homes of SpongeBob, Patrick, and Squidward.
  • City planning: Despite its unusual inhabitants and setting, Bikini Bottom is structured like an average American city, with a government, educational system, and law enforcement.
