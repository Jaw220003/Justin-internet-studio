Creative Brief

Problem

Our brand is struggling to differentiate itself in a saturated wellness market, leading to flat growth and low retention.

Audience

Health-conscious millennials aged 25–35 living in urban areas, who prioritize transparency, sustainability, and scientifically-backed products.

Competition

Main competitors include brands like Ritual and Care/of, who leverage personalized experiences, influencer partnerships, and subscription models effectively.

Key Messages

  • All-natural ingredients supported by clinical research
  • Full transparency on sourcing and production
  • Simple, daily routines to improve long-term health

Call to Action

Encourage users to take a personalized wellness quiz and sign up for a 30-day free trial of our supplement program.

How to Measure Success

  • 20% increase in new user sign-ups within 60 days
  • 15% increase in engagement across Instagram and email campaigns
  • Click-through rate (CTR) of at least 3.5% on paid ads
